Slite is a collaborative writing tool for the remote workplace, and is used by thousands of teams on a daily basis.

I helped the team to ship dozens of features and hundreds of small refinements. As an early employee I also contributed design direction to the overall product, brand, copywriting and content.

I wrote a bit about our process and how we worked at Slite <a rel="noreferrer" className="text-primary" href="https://miro.com/blog/product-development-slite/" target="_blank">here</a>
